What is Paths Games?

Paths Games is a modern digital gamebook, inspired by classic choose-your-own-adventure books but designed for the web and mobile devices.
Experience engaging stories, choose different paths, and face epic challenges alone or with friends.

Which path will you choose?
Enter the cave and face the dragon, or head toward the mysterious castle!

Engaging Stories

Fun, captivating narratives full of twists and unexpected outcomes.
